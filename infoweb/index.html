About BusQuest

BusQuest is an innovative mobile app designed to make your commuting experience more efficient and enjoyable. It allows you to check bus stop traffic and helps you avoid crowded stops through a gamified system that rewards you for choosing less busy times.

Features

  • See bus stop crowd levels based on historical data
  • Gamified rewards for visiting less crowded times
  • Engaging and user-friendly interface
  • Helps reduce waiting time and improve the commuting experience

How It Works

BusQuest uses historical data and patterns to predict bus stop crowding. The app provides suggestions for less busy times and rewards users for consistently choosing these times, making their commutes smoother and more efficient.
